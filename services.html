Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you specialize in for staffing solutions?

We specialize in providing skilled manpower solutions primarily for pharmaceutical and allied industries, including manufacturing, engineering, and facility management sectors.

How do you ensure compliance with statutory regulations?

Our payroll management team handles all statutory compliance including PF, ESI, Bonus, and Gratuity, ensuring all legal requirements are met and audits are supported.

Can you provide temporary and contract staffing services?

Yes, we offer contract and temporary staffing solutions tailored to your project needs, ensuring flexibility and skilled manpower availability.

What kind of training programs do you provide for new hires?

We conduct onboarding, induction, and skill training programs to ensure that new hires are well-prepared and aligned with your company’s standards and safety protocols.

How do you handle facility and housekeeping staff deployment?

We deploy trained facility and housekeeping staff to maintain cleanliness and operational efficiency at your premises, ensuring a safe and hygienic environment.

What is your approach to skilled manpower for MEP works?

We provide skilled manpower specialized in Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) works to support your infrastructure and maintenance projects efficiently.

How can I get in touch for a customized staffing solution?

You can contact us via the Contact page or directly chat with us on WhatsApp using the floating button on the bottom right corner of the page for quick assistance.
